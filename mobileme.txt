Mobile me was initially released on January 5, 2000, branded as Itools,
it was later rebranded to dot Mac in 2002 and then finally being changed to the iconic Mobile Me in 2008, 
It is a discontinued subscription-based collection of online services and software offered by Apple Incorporated.
All services were gradually transitioned to and eventually replaced by the free iCloud, and MobileMe ceased on June 30, 2012.
